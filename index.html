About Me

I have been a big fan of Human Computer Interaction (HCI) field since I was a sophomore. When I was a student, I designed and built interactive systems to support online learning and conducted research to understand how do those systems affect people. Now, I am pursuing a more generalized goal, that is, making better products to support social interaction and learning.
